# StratProof Lab Commercial License — Draft Terms

This document is a draft commercial-license framework for StratProof Lab Pro, Enterprise, and SaaS deployments.

A commercial license is **not automatically granted** by this repository. Commercial rights require a separate written agreement signed or otherwise approved by the project owner.

## Commercial license may cover

- private use without AGPL source-sharing obligations;
- hosted SaaS deployments;
- enterprise connectors;
- premium dashboard modules;
- internal strategy audit pipelines;
- private evidence reports;
- white-label or branded deployments;
- support, onboarding, training, and integration services.

## Commercial license does not automatically cover

- use of the StratProof Lab name or marks outside the trademark policy;
- resale as an official product without written approval;
- trading execution modules;
- financial-advice services;
- managed accounts;
- guaranteed-return marketing;
- use of private data, private models, or private infrastructure not explicitly licensed.

## Audit-first positioning

StratProof Lab is an audit and research platform. It is not a broker, exchange, investment adviser, or guaranteed trading system.

## Execution modules

Broker execution, live signal publishing, copy-trading, managed accounts, and automated order placement are outside the default Community scope. Any future commercial execution-related work would require separate terms, compliance review, and explicit written authorization.

## Warranty and liability

Commercial deployments should use explicit warranty, support, liability, uptime, data, and confidentiality terms in the signed agreement. This draft does not create those terms by itself.
