# StratProof Lab Trademark and Brand Policy

The code may be available under an open-source license, but the name, logo, icons, visual identity, screenshots, and product marks are not automatically licensed for unrestricted commercial use.

## Allowed without separate permission

You may use the StratProof Lab name to truthfully describe the original project, link to the official repository, identify compatibility, or state that your project is a fork.

Examples:

- "Forked from StratProof Lab"
- "Compatible with StratProof Lab Community Edition"
- "Uses the StratProof Lab audit format"

## Not allowed without written permission

You may not:

- present a fork as the official StratProof Lab product;
- use the StratProof Lab name in a competing commercial product name;
- use confusingly similar branding;
- imply endorsement or partnership;
- sell a SaaS or hosted service as official StratProof Lab without permission;
- use screenshots, logos, or visual marks in a misleading way.

## Fork naming rule

Forks should use a clearly different name and include a notice such as:

> This project is based on StratProof Lab Community Edition but is not affiliated with or endorsed by the StratProof Lab project owner.

## Internal legacy names

Private legacy research-system names are not public product branding and should not be used in forks or marketing.
