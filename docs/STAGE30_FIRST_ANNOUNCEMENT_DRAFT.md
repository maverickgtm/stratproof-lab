# First Announcement Draft

## Short version

StratProof Lab is now available as a community preview.

Most trading strategies look good — until they are audited.

StratProof Lab is an evidence engine for trading strategies. It helps traders, quants, and teams test ideas, detect weak backtests, compare evidence quality, and generate reports before risking capital.

This is not another signal bot. It is an audit layer for strategy research.

## Longer version

Today I am releasing the first community preview of StratProof Lab: an open-source evidence engine for trading strategies.

The goal is simple: help traders stop trusting pretty backtests and start demanding evidence.

StratProof Lab lets you build a strategy idea, connect or import market data, run an audit workflow, generate evidence cards, and inspect how the research system works through a visual Research Brain View.

The project starts with crypto-focused workflows and is designed to expand toward multi-market auditing for forex, equities, futures, and institutional connectors.

Community Edition is AGPL-licensed and audit-only by design.

## Closing line

Don’t pay for another strategy. First, find out if the strategy deserves trust.
