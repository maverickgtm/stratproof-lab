# Stage 26 — Founder Offer

## Purpose

The founder offer should validate demand without overcommitting permanent infrastructure costs.

## Recommended offer

**Founding Pro Year**

- $199 for the first year
- limited to first 100 customers
- renews at standard Pro annual price unless extended manually
- includes Pro Early Access features
- includes private feedback channel
- includes founder badge in account metadata

## Alternative offer

**Founding Lifetime**

- $199–$299 lifetime for first 50 customers

This is attractive but riskier because support obligations can grow forever. Prefer discounted first-year unless cashflow urgency is high.

## Sales message

> Become an early StratProof founder and help shape the evidence engine that trading systems are missing.

## Boundaries

The founder offer should not promise:

- profitable strategies
- signals
- managed trading
- guaranteed returns
- unlimited enterprise connectors
- lifetime infrastructure-heavy SaaS access unless explicitly scoped
