# Stage 20 — Screenshot Storyboard

## Purpose

The GitHub page and future homepage should sell the product visually, not just technically.

## Screenshot order

### 1. Hero screenshot — Formula Builder UI

Message: Build a trading idea without writing strategy code first.

Caption:

> Turn a trading idea into a structured research hypothesis.

### 2. Evidence Report Builder

Message: The product produces audit results, not just raw backtest tables.

Caption:

> See verdict, honest winrate, Net R, drawdown, leakage risk, duplicate risk, and evidence score.

### 3. Research Brain View

Message: This is not a simple backtest script. It is a research system.

Caption:

> Watch ideas move through the Research University, Truth Engine, Quality Gates, and Evidence Reports.

### 4. Data Setup / Provider Layer

Message: Users can bring data from exchanges, providers, or CSV.

Caption:

> Import crypto data today. Expand toward multi-market data tomorrow.

### 5. Research Cache / Saved Ideas

Message: Research should be reusable and comparable.

Caption:

> Save ideas, compare evidence, and avoid repeating the same research.

## Visual direction

- Dark, premium, analytical.
- Avoid “casino” feeling.
- Avoid fake profit promises.
- Use language around evidence, confidence, audit, and quality gates.
- Research Brain View can be visually futuristic, but the operational screens should stay clean and trustworthy.
