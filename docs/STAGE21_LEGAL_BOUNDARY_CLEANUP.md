# Stage 21 Legal Boundary Cleanup

## Public claims allowed

StratProof Lab may be described as:

- strategy audit platform;
- evidence engine;
- research-first tool;
- backtest quality gate;
- audit-only Community Edition;
- multi-market audit framework roadmap.

## Claims to avoid

Avoid claiming:

- promises of profitability;
- trading advice;
- investment advice;
- automatic income;
- managed accounts;
- official broker/exchange status;
- official partnership with exchanges or data vendors unless signed;
- live execution unless separately reviewed and licensed.

## Public wording

Use:

> StratProof Lab audits trading strategy evidence before capital is risked.

Avoid:

> StratProof Lab gives winning signals.

Use:

> Evidence before action.

Avoid:

> Guaranteed winrate.

## Brand separation

The public product is `StratProof Lab`. OpenClaw-related names remain internal legacy context and should not be used as public marketing.
