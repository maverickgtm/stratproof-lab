# Stage 54 — Annual Billing and Founding Member Discount Update

## Purpose

Clarify StratProof Lab pricing before GitHub publication so users understand that Community is free now and paid editions are planned later.

## Pricing policy

- Monthly billing remains flexible.
- Standard annual billing is planned to save about **15%** versus monthly billing.
- Founding Member annual billing is planned for the **first 100 annual subscribers** at about **40% off**.
- Enterprise remains custom.

## Planned price table

| Plan | Monthly | Standard annual | Founding annual |

## Repository safety

No active checkout, payment secrets, wallet private keys, customer data, or license signing keys are included in the Community repository.
