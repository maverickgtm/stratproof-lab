# Stage 20 — Competitor Positioning Notes

This document is not a legal or exhaustive competitor analysis. It is a product-positioning guide for how StratProof Lab should stand apart.

## Important competitors / references

### Freqtrade

Positioning: open-source crypto trading bot with exchange support, WebUI control, backtesting, plotting, money management, and strategy optimization.

Lesson: Freqtrade is strong because it makes the bot workflow practical and complete.

StratProof difference: We are not trying to be another bot. We are the audit layer before a bot or strategy deserves trust.

### Hummingbot

Positioning: open-source framework for automated trading strategies across centralized and decentralized exchanges.

Lesson: Hummingbot sells ecosystem, venues, connectors, and strategy deployment.

StratProof difference: We do not lead with deployment. We lead with research quality, evidence, and risk review.

### QuantConnect / LEAN

Positioning: professional multi-asset algorithmic trading platform and engine.

Lesson: QuantConnect is strong because it feels institutional and broad.

StratProof difference: We should not pretend to out-QuantConnect QuantConnect. Our wedge is the evidence-audit story: prove the idea, expose fragility, generate a report.

### VectorBT

Positioning: high-performance vectorized research/backtesting engine.

Lesson: VectorBT owns speed and large parameter sweeps.

StratProof difference: We do not sell speed as the primary value. We sell evidence quality and audit discipline.

### StockSharp

Positioning: broad trading platform with apps, connectors, and strategy infrastructure.

Lesson: StockSharp sells breadth and professional tooling.

StratProof difference: We can grow into many connectors, but our unique story is not breadth alone. It is strategy due diligence.

## What competitors generally do well

- Build strategies.
- Backtest strategies.
- Optimize parameters.
- Connect to exchanges/brokers.
- Deploy bots or live algorithms.
- Provide fast research frameworks.

## The gap StratProof should own

Most tools can tell you what performed well.

StratProof should tell you whether the evidence is trustworthy.

## Unique product language

Use these terms consistently:

- Evidence Engine
- Truth Engine
- Quality Gates
- Research University
- Strategy Due Diligence
- Audit-only by design
- Evidence before action
- Pretty backtests are not proof

## Avoid these weak messages

- Just another crypto backtester
- AI trading bot
- Automated signal generator
- High winrate system
- Guaranteed strategy builder

## Strong positioning line

Other tools help you build strategies.

StratProof Lab helps you challenge them.
