# Stage 26 — Sales Copy

## Main headline

Stop trusting pretty backtests. Demand evidence.

## Short pitch

StratProof Lab turns trading ideas into audited evidence reports. It checks whether a strategy is supported by real evidence or just looks good because of weak testing, inflated winrate, leakage, duplicates, or cherry-picked market regimes.

## One-liner

StratProof Lab is the evidence layer that other trading systems are missing.

## For individual traders

You do not need another magic strategy. You need to know whether your idea survives honest testing.

## For quant researchers

Build hypotheses, import market data, run chronological audits, compare regimes, and generate evidence reports without turning every idea into a custom research project.

## For trading communities

Show members what passed, what failed, what needs more data, and why. Replace hype with evidence.

## For enterprises

Deploy a private audit layer for strategy validation, research governance, connector intake, and evidence reporting across teams.

## Not a bot

StratProof Lab is not a signal bot and does not promise returns. It is a research and evidence platform designed to help users audit strategies before risking capital.
