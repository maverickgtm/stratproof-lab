# Stage 26 — Pricing Strategy

## Positioning

StratProof Lab is not monetized as a signal bot. It is monetized as an evidence engine for traders, quants, educators, communities, and trading teams.

The commercial promise is not “we give profitable trades.” The commercial promise is:

> Audit the evidence before you trust the strategy.

## Recommended pricing ladder

| Plan | Suggested price | Primary customer | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Community | $0 | open-source users, students, researchers | adoption, trust, GitHub growth |
| Pro Early Access | $29/mo, $295/yr standard annual, $209/yr Founding annual | serious individual traders | validate willingness to pay |
| Pro Plus | $79/mo, $805/yr standard annual, $569/yr Founding annual | power users and small quant operators | deeper reports and multi-market workflows |
| Team | $199/mo, $2,030/yr standard annual, $1,433/yr Founding annual | trading communities, academies, groups | shared workspace and branded reports |
| Enterprise / SaaS | from $999/mo or custom quote | funds, prop firms, brokers, data teams | private deployment, premium connectors, white-label |

## Why this ladder works

Community should be generous enough to earn trust. Pro should unlock convenience, scale, and advanced evidence workflows. Team should monetize collaboration. Enterprise should monetize custom data, compliance, and deployment requirements.

## Founding offer

Optional early-access offer:

- Founding Pro: $199 lifetime for first 50–100 users, or
- Founding Year: $199 first year, then renews at normal annual price.

The safer option is a discounted first year, not lifetime, because support and infrastructure costs can grow.

## Pricing principle

StratProof Lab should charge for confidence, workflow, reporting, and scale — not for hype.
