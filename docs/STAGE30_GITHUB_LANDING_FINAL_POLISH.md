## Positioning

StratProof Lab is not positioned as another bot, signal system, or generic backtester.

It is positioned as:

> An evidence engine for trading strategies.

The core message:

> Most trading strategies look good — until they are audited.

## Public promise

StratProof Lab helps traders, quants, educators, and teams turn trading ideas into evidence reports before risking capital.

It focuses on:

- honest winrate
- Net R
- drawdown
- duplicate risk
- leakage risk
- evidence quality
- multi-timeframe context
- strategy audit workflow
- visual research observability

## Public boundaries

StratProof Lab Community is audit-only by design.
It does not promise returns, provide financial advice, manage accounts, or place broker orders in the Community edition.
