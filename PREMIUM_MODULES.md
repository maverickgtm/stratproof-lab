# Premium / Pro / Enterprise Module Boundary

The public Community Edition is designed to prove the core idea: strategy evidence should be audited before capital is risked.

Potential premium modules may include:

- enterprise connector packs;
- advanced data lineage and evidence scoring;
- private team dashboards;
- SaaS-hosted Research University;
- larger multi-market data imports;
- institutional data adapters;
- white-label reports;
- private strategy libraries;
- advanced Quality Gate packs;
- commercial support and onboarding.

Premium modules should not be committed into the public Community repository unless intentionally released under the Community license.
