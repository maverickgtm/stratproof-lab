# StratProof Lab — Final Public Name Decision

**Final public name:** StratProof Lab  
**Short name:** StratProof  
**Repository name recommendation:** `stratproof-lab`  
**Product category:** Evidence engine for trading strategies  

## Positioning

StratProof Lab is not a bot, not a signal channel, and not a promise machine. It is an audit-first research framework that helps traders, quants, educators, and trading teams test whether a strategy has real evidence before risking capital.

## Core line

> Stop trusting pretty backtests. Demand evidence.

## Brand boundary

The former internal project name remains private/legacy. StratProof Lab is the public brand used for the Community Edition, documentation, screenshots, and GitHub presentation.
