StratProof Lab Community Preview is ready.

This project is built around a simple belief: a strategy should not be trusted just because a backtest looks good.

StratProof Lab helps turn trading ideas into auditable evidence. It includes a Formula Builder, Provider Connector Layer, Evidence Report cards, Research Brain View, multilingual interface foundations, pricing boundaries, licensing boundaries, and a one-command public demo.

It starts with crypto strategy auditing and is designed to grow into a multi-market audit framework.

Audit-only by design. Evidence before action.
