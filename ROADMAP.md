# Roadmap

## Available Now
- Crypto strategy audit workflows
- Public OHLCV connectors for Bybit, Binance, OKX, Coinbase Exchange, and Kraken
- Duplicate-safe cache writes and completed-candle safeguards
- CSV import
- Formula Builder
- Measured Evidence Reports and unrestricted audit-trail CSV exports
- Research Brain View for workflow observability

## Next Research Gates
- Import external trade ledgers from TradingView, Freqtrade and generic CSV formats
- Add configurable fees, spread, slippage and funding sensitivity analysis
- Add holdout and walk-forward validation reports
- Measure concentration, lookahead and parameter-fragility diagnostics
- Expand provider-health and cross-exchange comparison reports

## Later
- More public/import connectors for crypto, forex, stocks, futures and options, subject to data rights and test coverage
- Reproducible public case studies contributed by the community
- Optional hosted collaboration only if community demand makes it worthwhile
